Our Projects

Project Vriddhi

 Aimed at reducing the contribution of stubble burning to pollution in India, Vriddhi puts stubble to uses like mushroom cultivation, setting up community washrooms, and making handicrafts. 

Project Ahsaas

 Project Ahsaas aims to socially and economically uplift the visually impaired and provide digital literacy to the children belonging to low-income groups. 

Project Armaan

 Project Armaan at Enactus Hansraj aims at making past drug addicts future entrepreneurs, by running development programs, setting up rehabilitation centers and product lines to be managed by the target community.
